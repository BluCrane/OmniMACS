Why should you use OmniM*A*C*S?

OmniM*A*C*S offers unique value within the business support industry. We combine digital media skills, creative talent and proven business development expertise in one dynamic package that guarantees the biggest bang for your buck.

We are committed to empowering our clients to make the most cost-effective and productive decisions with regards to their marketing, administration and communication needs.

So who are we?

OmniM*A*C*S was founded by Marc Steyn, a freelance writer and editor, with a background in international travel management, hospitality and business development. The goal was to establish a one-stop shop for affordable, but high quality, business support services.

Based in Cape Town our digital focus gives us a global scope with a South African perspective.
