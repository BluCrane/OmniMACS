Why should you use OmniMACS?

OmniMACS offers unique value within the business support industry. We combine digital media skills, creative talent and proven business development expertise in one dynamic package that guarantees the biggest bang for your buck.

We are committed to empowering our clients to make the most cost-effective and productive decisions with regards to their marketing, administration and communication needs.

Based in Cape Town our digital focus gives us a global scope with a South African perspective.

So who are we?

OmniMACS was founded by Marc Steyn, a freelance writer and digital media specialist, with an award-winning background in business development, travel management and the hospitality industry. The aim was to establish a one-stop shop for affordable, but high quality, business support services.

We rely on the eclectic skills of carefully-selected niche consultants to produce results of the highest calibre. Our closely knit group of professionals includes experienced business leaders, published authors and established digital media experts.
